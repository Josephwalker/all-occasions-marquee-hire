Order Process

Step one 

You can contact us initially with your marquee hire enquiry either by email, using the making an enquiry section on this website or by giving us a call. We’ll discuss your ideas and then provide you with an estimate to hire a marquee, based on the information you have supplied.

Step two

If your happy with the quote we will arrange to visit you at the site where your marquee is to be located. We will review the site and take all the necessary measurements and photographs.

During the visit we will offer you guidance to help you decide on the marquee size, and layout that is best suited to your requirements. We will also discuss with you some ideas on how you would like the marquee interior decorating and what other furniture and accessories you might require.

Step three

Once we have agreed a specification with you, we will produce a final quotation detailing your main requirements and any additional items that you may want.

If you decide to proceed with a booking, we require a non-refundable deposit of 20% + vat, of the total amount. 

If you want to vary some aspect of the marquee layout at a later date that’s fine you can do this up to three weeks before the event. The Outstanding balance must be paid 10 days prior to the event date.

Step four

Construction and removal of your marquee will be on pre-arranged days to suit you and us, we will contact you around 6 weeks before to arrange these dates, We always try and have the marquee up at least 24 hours before the event to give you time to add your own special touch.
