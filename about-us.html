Marquees

All our marquees are premium quality and are aluminium framed. The marquee roof covers and walls fit tightly into the aluminium frame ensuring that the marquee is weather tight and your guests are well protected from the elements.

Decoration

The interior decoration of your marquee is an important consideration as it can really help to create the ambience desired for your event. Whether you would like something simple and sophisticated or something grand and extravagant, we can help by creating a decorative scheme using curtains, drapes and room dividers.

Lighting

The type of lighting you select can complement your decorative scheme and further enhance the ambience of your event. We can supply and fit a wide variety of lighting styles including chandeliers, uplighters and spotlights. If your event will continue into the night, we can also provide exterior lighting to illuminate outside features.

Furniture

Whatever your event, we can supply furniture to suit. For formal weddings, we can install traditional tables and chairs or for an informal garden party, you may prefer sofas or patio-style furniture sets. For corporate events, you may require theatre-style seating for conferences and seminars, but bar stools with poseur tables may be preferred for exhibition areas. Whatever your preference, we can source and supply furniture to fit.

Dance Floors

If your event requires a dance floor, we can supply either a parquet floor or a black and white checked floor, which can be enhanced with a black starlight ceiling cloth. A lighting rig can also be installed if required to illuminate the dance floor with lights that operate to the beat of the music.

Electronics

Where there is a requirement to show photographs or play video footage, we can also supply plasma screens. They can be located anywhere within the marquee and linked to a laptop or DVD player as required.

Toilet Facilities

Whatever you event, you will no doubt require toilet facilities. We can supply luxury toilet units and link them to the main marquee with a covered walkway ensuring they are accessible whatever the weather.
